Why Us?

PaySwitch is a payment processing company that offers alternative banking solutions to banks, financial institutions, organizations, businesses, and individuals globally. Their integrated payment solutions enable the seamless circulation and exchange of money and values across various transactions (C2C, C2B, B2C, B2B).

Their target market includes commercial banks, non-banking financial institutions, telecommunication companies, and payment organizations in Ghana and West Africa. PaySwitch serves a wide range of users, from startups to large organizations, providing flexible systems, simplified programming interfaces, and negotiated rates through their incubator Payments program. Established in 2015, PaySwitch operates with world-class technology platforms, certified compliance with data protection regulations, and adherence to industry standards like ISO 27001 and PCI DSS.
They operate under the Payment Systems and Service Act, 2019 (Act 987).
